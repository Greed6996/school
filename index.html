About Lower Assam School of Nursing

Our School

Established with a vision to revolutionize healthcare education in Bongaigaon, our school provides a rigorous academic environment where students wear their white uniforms with pride and integrity. We are recognized by INC and MNC.

Parent Hospital

Clinical excellence is achieved through direct practice. Our students undergo extensive training at our parent multispecialty hospital, gaining real-world experience in critical care, pediatrics, and surgery departments.

Mrs. Salomi Indwar

Principal
Lower Assam School of Nursing

The Lower Assam School of Nursing has been established in the year 2015, under the banner of Lower Assam Hospital and Research Centre, Bongaigaon. The School has been affiliated to various state Govt. Hospital for their clinical field experience.

The objectives of Lower Assam School of Nursing is to provide quality nursing education through which the students will gain knowledge of quality nursing care for prevention of disease, promotion of health and rehabilitation also will have the knowledge of moral and ethical values in their professional and personal life.

Modern nursing is a dynamic, therapeutic and educative process in meeting the health need of individuals family and community, nursing is one of the health professional which function is conjunction with other health agencies in assisting individual, families and communities to achive and maintain desirable standard of health.

Indian Nursing Council recognized that basic nursing education is a formally recognized programme study providing broad and sound foundation in the behavior it believes that this basic course in nursing school prepare nursing for occupying first level position in the health care delivery system. The Council recognized that nursing is a profession, which is influenced by advances in science and technology. It believes that skills in all aspects of communication are also essential for learning and for the practice of nursing.
